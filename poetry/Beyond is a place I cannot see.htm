Beyond

 

 

Beyond the horizon is a place I have not seen

where the wine must run like water

the trees sway in breezes

streams flow over waterfalls.

It beckons to give me a home

while it chews at me, its sleeping tiger waking,

gnawing the remnants of its kill

viscera, marrow and muscle

until finally bored, it stretches

yawns and turns its eye to the hunt.

 

You think you have been there

you have walked amongst the forest

drank the wine, seen the river

watched the beast pad silently

like fog moving through underbrush and brambles

as you held your breath

hoping the wind would stay at your face.

 

You think you were there on moonlit nights

you felt the desire to prey

heard that last squeal, watched the life fade,

rejoiced as the light ebbed,

and your hands tore it to pieces.

 

Too bad this poem is not about you.

My soul is a worn thimble

the spears of needles its only trials

but enough to mark and leave a scar.

 

Beyond the horizon is a place I have not seen

where the wine must run like water.

 

 

                                    Bill Schreiber

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

the trees sway, streams flow.

 

I know it is nothing so melodramatic

It must be a forest like any other forest

trees, stones, the occasional stream

Still I have not seen it.

 

 

 

You say I should not go

the danger is obviously great

but I only want what I cannot have

to walk with the beast to become the hunter

 

There can only be so much light.

 

Who knows what its eyes see

I have never seen it so I could only guess.

 

 

No stars to portend or to light the way

answers are not what I am looking for.

I want to sleep, a restful dreamless sleep

I want to wake at noon once again

I want to rest, just rest.

 

There is no rest for the wicked or the rudderless

wander is destiny,  my soul a thimble

worn from use, the spears of needles its only trials,

but enough anyways to mark and leave a scar.

 

trees sway in a never ending breeze

that shushes my thoughts and lets me sleep

sleep beyond dreams deep and dark

it is my emollient, my peace
